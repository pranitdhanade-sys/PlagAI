Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. PlagAI is committed to protecting your personal information and uploaded documents. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data while providing AI plagiarism detection services.

Information We Collect

  • Name and email address during account registration.
  • Uploaded documents for plagiarism and AI-content analysis.
  • Basic usage analytics to improve the platform.
  • Device and browser information for security purposes.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to provide plagiarism detection, generate originality reports, improve our AI models, and enhance platform performance. We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers.

Document Security

Documents uploaded to PlagAI are processed securely. Access is restricted to authorized systems required for plagiarism analysis. We use encryption and secure storage practices to protect your files.

Cookies

Cookies help us remember your preferences, maintain secure sessions, and improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if preferred.

Third-Party Services

PlagAI may integrate trusted third-party authentication, cloud storage, or analytics services. These providers process data only as required to deliver their services and are expected to follow applicable privacy standards.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal information.
  • Update or correct account details.
  • Request deletion of your account.
  • Request deletion of uploaded documents where applicable.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain platform security.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the platform or by email where appropriate.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact the PlagAI support team.
