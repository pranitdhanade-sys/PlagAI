ABOUT PLAGAI

Building Trust in
Academic & AI Writing

PlagAI is designed to help students, educators, researchers, and organizations verify originality, identify AI-generated content, and produce reliable writing through fast, secure, and transparent analysis.

🎯 Our Mission

Empower people with accurate plagiarism detection and AI analysis tools that encourage authentic writing and academic integrity.

🚀 Our Vision

Create a platform where originality can be verified quickly, fairly, and securely for education, publishing, and professional content creation.

🛡️ Our Platform

PlagAI combines AI detection, plagiarism analysis, grammar assistance, citation generation, and reporting into one easy-to-use platform.

Our Story

As AI writing tools become increasingly common, verifying originality has become more challenging than ever. PlagAI was created to simplify that process by bringing multiple writing analysis tools together in a single platform.

Our goal is to build software that is fast, reliable, and accessible for students, educators, businesses, researchers, and content creators while maintaining privacy and transparency.
