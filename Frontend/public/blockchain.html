Project Synopsis

This project proposes a blockchain-based cryptocurrency optimized for day-to-day payments rather than speculative trading. The system employs a high-throughput consensus mechanism such as Proof of Stake (PoS) or Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS) to achieve low latency, high transaction throughput, and minimal energy consumption. Layer-2 scaling, sharding, and off-chain payment channels reduce transaction costs while maintaining security. Smart contracts automate merchant settlements, recurring payments, and digital identity verification. Mobile wallets support QR-code and NFC payments for seamless retail transactions. Public-key cryptography, digital signatures, and hashing provide security, while stable-value mechanisms minimize volatility. Banking interoperability and optional KYC/AML modules enable smooth fiat conversion and regulatory compliance, creating a scalable and user-friendly digital payment ecosystem.
